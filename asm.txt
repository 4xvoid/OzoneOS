Instructions:
	Data:
		STOR R0,VAL   | Store VAL At R0.
		INP R0        | Get User Input And Store At R0.
		LOAD R0,R1    | Copy R1 To R0.
	Math:            
		IF R0,=/!,VAL | Check If R0 =/! VAL.
		END           | End IF Statement.
		ADD R0,R1     | Add R1 To R0.
		SUB R0,R1     | Subtract R1 To R0.
		MUL R0,R1     | Multiply R1 To R0.
		DIV R0,R1     | Divide R1 To R0.
		INC R0        | Adds 1 To R0.
		DEC R0        | Subtracts 1 To R0.
	Grahics:
		PRNT R0      | Print R0 To Console Without New Line.
		PRNTL R0     | Print R0 To Console With New Line.
		CLR           | Clear Console.
		BCOL COLOR    | Change Background Color To COLOR.
		FCOL COLOR    | Change Foreground Color To COLOR.
	REQUIRMENTS:
		STOP          | Exits The Program.
